Introduction

The University of Salford organised along with probably the best theatre in town , a Jam dedicated to students. The goal of the event was to create a working game what would encourage people to explore more parts of the theatre while waiting, for example, for a movie to start.

Our idea

After brainstorming and checking up with the judges we decided to stick to this idea : Make a game that would require people to scan certain QR codes placed inside the building and award them if they complete a challenge. A mini game would appear on the screen when a QR code is scanned and after playing it an item would appear in the inventory. We thought that this would be an interactive way of making people explore their surroundings and have fun at the same time (something like pokemon go).

Our final product

After a whole weekend we were proud to say that we made a fully working game! It is nice to say what people create in just 3 days. We made a video with our game in action.
